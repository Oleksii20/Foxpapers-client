About us:

Brief history:

FoxPapers was founded in 2015 by three Engineering graduates who were convinced that maintaining life-study balance at university is a key to both effective academic work and personal development and growth.

Since then, they managed to put together a team of professionals in academic writing, experts recruitment, client support, quality assurance, and many others, who want to help students around the globe reach their goals in the chosen field.

Our main goal is to provide honest writing services with low commissions, as we value quality and customer satisfaction over profit.
